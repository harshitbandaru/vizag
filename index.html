Why Vizag is known as the city of destiny

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is called the "City of Destiny" for a number of reasons, including: The city's name is inspired by the Hindu god of courage, Visakha. The phrase "destiny favors the brave" is said to be the reason for the city's nickname. Visakhapatnam has a rich history that dates back over 2,500 years. It was an ancient port city with trade relations with Rome and the Middle East. Visakhapatnam is also known for its beaches, serene landscape, and cultural past. It's a popular destination for coastal vacations.
